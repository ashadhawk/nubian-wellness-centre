Cycling is an exciting way to improve fitness. However, black people are under-represented in the cycling community. Our spin classes are designed to encourage, nourish and promote an interest in cycling for fitness and leisure.

SPIN

At the Nubian Wellness Centre, we believe that swimming is an essential life skill. Evidence shows that over 60% of black youths cannot swim, thereby putting them at higher risk of drowning. Our swimming sessions and lessons are designed to promote water confidence and enhance swimming competence. .

SWIM

This is our signature activity and a favorite of our members. An aerobic whole body exercise session incorporating a unique dance experience to the rhythm and pulsating beats of afrobeats. Our dance classes are led by experienced instructors who infuse the latest afro dance steps into their routines to create an unforgetable experience.
