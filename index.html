Our Objectives

  • To promote an healthy and active lifestyle among black people in the diaspora.

  • To increase participation in exercise for health activities through the provision of an enabling environment that will engender regular exercise behaviour.

  • To act as advocate on behalf of black people to increase access to affordable physical activity and exercise facilities.

Welcome!

At the Nubian Wellness Centre, our ethos goes beyond being a fitness centre. Our goal is to enhance the health, well-being and quality of life of people of colour. We do this through the promotion of regular physical activities and exercise. There is overwhelming evidence of the benefit of regular physical activities. There is also considerable evidence that black people in western societies are less likely to engage in regular physical activity and exercise due a variety of factors. They, therefore miss out on the enormous benefits especially health-related, associated with physical activities and exercise. This makes them have a higher risk of developing inactivity-related diseases such as Obesity, Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus, Stroke and Cardiovascular Disease (Heart problems). Our goal at the Nubian Wellness Centre is to remedy that.
